The Ultimate Guide to Tempered Glass Shower Maintenance

Keep your tempered glass shower enclosure pristine with proper maintenance techniques. Daily care includes using a squeegee after each use, ensuring proper ventilation, and wiping down hardware. Weekly cleaning with a vinegar-water solution prevents soap scum and mineral buildup, while protecting hydrophobic coatings requires gentle, pH-neutral cleaners. Combat hard water deposits with specialized removers or water softener systems. Long-term care involves regular inspection of seals and hardware, with professional maintenance when needed. Following these guidelines, along with a consistent cleaning schedule, will extend your shower's life and maintain its sparkling appearance.
